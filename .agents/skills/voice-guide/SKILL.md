# Voice Guide Skill

## Overview
This skill enforces the unique editorial voice, epistemic stance, and copywriting constraints of the Hillary Njuguna brand. It ensures all user-facing content (essays, dispatches, digest entries, and research logs) reads as authentic human observation.

## Core Directives
When editing or authoring any content files in `src/content/` or `docs/`, you must load and strictly adhere to the rules defined in `docs/voice-guide.md`.

### 1. The Six Voice Signatures
Every piece of writing must integrate:
- **Meta-Narrator Interrupt**: Structured, offset blocks auditing the inquiry's methodology.
- **Held Contradiction**: Stating opposing realities simultaneously without resolving them.
- **Sensory Crack**: Intruding concrete physical or environmental details mid-analysis.
- **Shame Managed Through Compression**: Framing failure or vulnerability in dry, clinical jargon.
- **Class and Aspiration Subtext**: Reflecting the struggle to enter elite professional environments.
- **Calibrated Uncertainty**: Explicitly framing boundaries between trajectory and fact.

### 2. Epistemic Notebook Identity
- **Discovery Tense**: Use the first-person discovery tense; show the chronological path of realizing the thesis.
- **Laboratory Notebook**: Treat the essay as an experimental log documenting inputs, latencies, and methodologies.

### 3. Audience Progression
Structure content along the linear progression from C to B to A:
- **Audience C**: Framework Practitioner (CIR: Cognitive Infrastructure Readiness).
- **Audience B**: System Researcher (DCFB: Distributed Cognition as Foundational Behavior).
- **Audience A**: Sovereign Operator (Audits, Martha Cohorts).

### 4. Hard Constraints
- **No Em-Dashes**: Semicolons and colons must replace all em-dashes.
- **No Bullet Points in Prose**: Standalone lists only; do not mix bullet points inside standard running paragraphs.
- **No AI Buzzwords**: Banish words like delve, tapestry, testament, revolutionize, and leverage.
