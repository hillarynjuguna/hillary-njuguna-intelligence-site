# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** 2026-05-10

Oscillatory Fields provides the Cognitive Infrastructure Readiness (CIR) Assessment and related sovereign cognitive architecture platforms. 

## 1. Information Collection
When you use the CIR Assessment or any of our diagnostic funnels, we collect:
- **Contact Information:** Email address (for delivering results).
- **Assessment Data:** Responses to diagnostic questions regarding your institutional architecture, governance structures, and cognitive infrastructure.

## 2. Use of Information
We use this information exclusively to:
- Generate and deliver your custom CIR Assessment report.
- Synthesize macro-level insights for the Oscillatory Fields research corpus (anonymized).

## 3. Data Protection and Provenance
We employ the Constitutional Provenance Envelope (CPE) framework where applicable to guarantee the integrity of your telemetry. Your assessment data is stored securely and never sold to third parties.

## 4. Contact
For any privacy-related inquiries or to request data deletion, contact the $\tau$-node at Oscillatory Fields.
