## The Procurement Interpretation Gap

In most procurement processes, the gap between what organisations intend to receive and what they actually receive is a structure problem, not a communication problem.

Vendors interpret requirements through the lens of what they can deliver. Procurement teams write requirements through the lens of what they know to ask for. The intersection of these two lenses is the interpretation gap — and it is where most procurement failures originate.

ClearBid applies structured intelligence to reduce this gap systematically.
