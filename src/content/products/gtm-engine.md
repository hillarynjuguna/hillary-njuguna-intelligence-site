The **Activation Intelligence Engine** (codenamed `GTM_Engine`) is the first commercial deployment of the **Lexicon #4** framework. It transforms passive business data into a sovereign, high-intent execution layer.

### Core Capabilities

Designed specifically for the complexities of the Malaysian F&B landscape, the engine provides:

1.  **Sovereign E-Invoicing**: Direct, local-first integration with LHDN MyInvois Portal, bypassing centralized third-party aggregators to maintain business privacy.
2.  **WhatsApp Command Layer**: Natural language interaction for order management, inventory queries, and financial reporting. No complex dashboards; just conversational intelligence.
3.  **Local-First SQL Substrate**: Data is owned and stored in the merchant's local environment, ensuring that the "Memory Layer" of the business resides on-site.

### Why It Matters

Traditional ERP systems are "Recording Systems"—they store what happened. The **Activation Intelligence Engine** is a **Doing System**.

By applying **Calibration Layers** to every WhatsApp request, the engine ensures that invoices are generated accurately and in strict compliance with current Malaysian tax regulations, while providing the merchant with real-time insights that were previously locked in static spreadsheets.

### Status

The engine is currently operational and being deployed to selected F&B partners in the region. It represents the intersection of agentic reasoning (Lexicon) and physical-world execution (Engine).
